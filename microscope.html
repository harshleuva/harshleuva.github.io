1800x Microscope

A custom-built high-magnification microscope, assembled to explore the microscopic world beyond what the naked eye can see.

The Goal

The main goal was to build a powerful optical microscope from a kit to understand the principles of optics, including how lenses work together to magnify an image, and to achieve a high level of magnification (1800x) for observing cellular structures.

My Process & Learnings

  • Assembled the microscope from a component kit, including the eyepiece, objective lenses, and focusing mechanism.
  • Learned to properly calibrate the lenses and lighting for clear imaging.
  • Prepared my own slides with samples like onion skin and cheek cells.
  • Mastered the fine and coarse adjustment knobs to bring microscopic details into sharp focus.

Outcome & Reflection

The microscope is fully functional and capable of providing clear images at high magnifications. This project was a fantastic, hands-on introduction to the world of microscopy and the hidden details of biology.
