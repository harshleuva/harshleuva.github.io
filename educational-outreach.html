Sharing the Spark of Science

A journal of my experiences visiting local communities to conduct fun, hands-on learning sessions with young, curious minds.

August 12, 2025
Local Community Basti, Pune

Visit 1: An Open Classroom

For my first outreach, my group visited a local basti to create a welcoming learning space. Instead of a fixed lesson plan, we asked the children what they were curious about. Some wanted help with math, others with reading. We tailored our approach to each child, creating a flexible and dynamic teaching environment that responded directly to their needs.
