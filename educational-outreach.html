Sharing the Spark of Science

A journal of my experiences visiting local communities to conduct fun, hands-on learning sessions with young, curious minds.

August 12, 2025
Local Community Basti, Pune

Visit 1: An Open Classroom

For my first outreach, my group visited a local basti to create a welcoming learning space. Instead of a fixed lesson plan, we asked the children what they were curious about. Some wanted help with math, others with reading. We tailored our approach to each child, creating a flexible and dynamic teaching environment that responded directly to their needs.

August 25, 2025
Local Community Basti, Pune

Visit 2: Same as previous one

Building on the success of our first visit, we returned to find the children eager to learn with us again. This time, we introduced a specific hands-on activity: building simple electric circuits with batteries and LEDs. The moment of pure joy when a child successfully lit up their bulb for the first time was incredible. This session reinforced the power of interactive experiments to make abstract concepts tangible and fun.

September 19, 2025
Local Community Basti, Pune

Visit 3: Same as previous one

Some of the children become my friend and also they learned many new thing from me and also I introduced them to some new good habits like (drinking 2 bottles of water everyday, not eating packet food too often) which they surprisingly followed.
