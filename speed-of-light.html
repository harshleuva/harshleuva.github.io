Speed of Light Experiment

An ambitious attempt to measure one of the universe's fundamental constants using accessible materials.

Work In Progress!

This is a highly experimental and ongoing project. I'm currently in the research and design phase, exploring different methods to measure the speed of light, such as using a modulated laser and a detector. The goal is to build a setup that is precise enough to get a reasonably accurate measurement. Check back later for updates on my progress!
